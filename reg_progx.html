MechMania IV Registration Form

2-4 October, 1998

Please read rules before registering. A maximum of 16 teams can register. Teams are added on a first come first serve basis. Prefrence will be given to teams outside the University of Illinois. There is a preset limit of the amount of local teams that can register.

Currently we have more then 16 teams and are not accepting any more local (UIUC) teams. But if you are out of state please register due to the fact that several teams may drop, or not meet final selection


Final selection is determined the by team size, returning team or not, location of team (in state or not), and order of registration. Selected teams will be posted on the page Sunday the 26th and E-mailed. All mailed teams MUST rsvp to the email to stay selected.
