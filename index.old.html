Student Computing Conference
October 2-4, 1998

About the Conference

The Association for Computing Machinery at the University of Illinois is hosting its fourth annual student computing conference on October 2-4 of this year. Registration for the conference is open to anyone and everyone who is interested in cutting edge computing technology. No university or ACM affiliation is required. Online registration will be available in the next few weeks.

Programming Contest

MechMania returns for a fourth year of fun and galactic conquest. The MechMania pages will soon have information about the fourth incarnation of this object-oriented programming contest. The mechanized warriors will return from deep space for a ground battle of incredible proportions.

Employment Fair

Conference activities begin on October 2 with a day-long employment fair, which is not restricted to registered conference attendees. If you are interested in recruiting at the conference, online registration will be available soon, but please mail the conference committee in the mean time.

Get involved!

As an attendee:
  • Online registration will be available soon.
As a speaker:
  • If you would like to give a presentation about the past, present, or future of computing, please mail the conference committee .
As part of an ACM chapter:
  • Represent your school at the annual Midwest chapters meeting.
  • Be a reflector partner for the live webcast of the conference.
As a corporate sponsor :
  • Online registration is now available.
  • Recruit at the employment fair.
  • Donate equipment.
  • Mail our corporate liaison with questions.

  • Take charge of one of the many positions available within the planning committee:
    Social Coordinator,
    Registration Coordinator,
    Extra Event Coordinator,
    WebCast Producer,
    ACM Chapter Liaison
