The registration fee is $15, which entitles you to attend all conference events. It also includes most of your meals for the weekend and a swanky t-shirt.

     If you are the representative of an ACM chapter and you would like to attend the annual Midwest chapters meeting, please mail reflections@acm.uiuc.edu after you register.

     Free housing with UIUC students is available if you don't mind sleeping on floors and couches. Check the box under Personal Information if you would like us to find you a place to stay. Discounted hotel accomodations are also available.

     The deadline for sending resumes to the conference committee for inclusion in the resume book will be approximately three weeks before the conference. If you turn your resume in when you get to the conference, it will be included in a supplemental resume book that is mailed to all of our sponsors after the conference.

     If you are planning to participate in MechMania IV, please register here first, and then register at the MechMania page.

     Registration as a Volunteer is now closed. Information for those already registered is here.

     Please pay at the registration booth when you arrive. If your payment is received three weeks before the start of the conference, you will be entered into a drawing for a book from O'Reilly & Associates. There are several prizes available, including software from Microsoft.
