Procmail Workshop - Joe Gross

Abstract

Is your inbox swamped? Do you receive hundreds of messages a day from mailing lists? Are you tired of collecting spam? Do you want to automatically organize your incoming mail? Do you want to run mailing lists from your normal account?

If you've ever had problems managing your email then procmail might be the tool for you. You'll learn the basics from how to install procmail to simple filtering to more complex automation.


Biography

Joe Gross is a Trusted Systems Engineer with Argus Systems Group in Savoy, Illinois. He spends his day developing trusted operating systems and researching computer security issues. Joe has also done systems administration for the public UNIX systems at the U of I. When he's not doing "real work" he enjoys reading, cycling, and martial arts training. He may be reached at jgross@jgross.net.
